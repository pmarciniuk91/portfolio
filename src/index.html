About Me

Hello I'm Pawel Marciniuk Frontend Developer Cv Contact Me

I completed an Engineer's degree in faculty of Electrotechnics at Lublin University of Technology in Poland. One year of my studies I spent abroad in Spain which gave me an unique opportunity of finding myself in an international environment. I always try to look for inspiration everywhere I am.

Once I finished university I left home for a half-year solo hitchhiking trip around Europe and Mexico. During my travel I learnt how to face stressful situations and solve problems myself. My good English and Spanish skills give me opportunity to look at things from other points of view and are very helpful to communicate well with people all around the world.

I have the ability to adapt to working as part of a development team. Also I consider myself a quick learner with good analytical skills. I am ready, willing, and able to put in extra time to do the job well.
